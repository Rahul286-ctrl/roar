Vision

Today in Indian elections voters doesn’t have information about the candidate who get ticket from a party and will represent that from a specific region. There can be a several types of information includes :

  • Personal information includes name , age , qualification, profession, political career etc.
  • Budget sanctioned by the government to state and expenditure according to Mplad and Mlalad.
  • Budget sanctioned by the state government to different constituency and expenditure. And percentage utilized.
  • Performace of the government at national and state level.
  • Interaction between MP’s and MLA’s with their public of their elected constituency.

Roar is a website. It provides the details about the candidate who has been elected as MP or MLA from their constituency . It works like an advanced wikipedia for the voters about the MP and MLA from their constituency. It does not only deals with personal details about the candidate but also provides the information about the work that has been done by him. The details which it provides also helps at the time of election for voters .It tells the voters that the candidate the want to choose what qualities he has and on the basis of qualities voter choose the right candidate . It also has a online polling system with the help of which they can get an idea that which candidate is going to win from their constituency during the election. It also provides the details about the fund that has been released for the constituency by the government and how much has beem used . So, this plateform will be a place which will help the voters of India to choose right candidate.
